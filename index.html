Application of Information and Communication Technologies

This course is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge of communication technologies. Apart from leading the participants through a brief history of computing, the course also develops a feel for web programming through a series of lectures that help the students develop their own web page. Main objective of the course is to build an appreciation for the fundamental concepts in computing and to become familiar with popular PC productivity software.

Course Information:

Duration: 16 weeks

Prerequisites: Basic knowledge of programming and computer systems.

Topics Covered:
  1. Introduction to ICT
  2. Computer Architecture
  3. Database Management
  4. Network Topologies
  5. SDLC
  6. Web Development
